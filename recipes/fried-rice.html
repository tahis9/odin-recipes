Fried Rice

This one isn't a real recipe

Description

Fried Rice made by pre-cooking ingredients. Pre-cooked rice is put into a wok and ingredients added into the wok to make Fried Rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Meat (eg. Char Siu)
  • Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Dice Meat and Vegetables
  3. Stir-fry meat and vegetables separately
  4. Add salt to eggs while whisking them
  5. Add cooked rice to wok
  6. Pour eggs on the rice and stir
  7. Add meat and vegetables
  8. Pour Soy Sauce after the eggs are fully cooked, stir and let simmer
  9. Serve :P
